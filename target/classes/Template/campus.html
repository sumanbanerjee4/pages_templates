College Campus

The college campus of Sharat College of Engineering, Panipat is meticulously planned and is setup at 26 acres land with state of the art buildings and infrastructure in the foot steps of historical state Haryana.
The campus is having 1,00,000 sq. ft. covered area. The College Campus divided into functional zones like Hostels, main College Buliding, Administration Block and a sports complex.

The campus is well equipped with well-planned building having facilities like well lit lecture halls, tutorial rooms, drawing halls, laboratories, computer centre, library, canteen, hostel, sports complex. The campus is having lush green lawns and landscaping is done by experts.
